About Amit

I built my first website when I was 14. It was terrible. I like to think I've improved - over the past three years, my web properties have served over one million pageviews.

I've meandered around technology ever since. At 19 I launched a tech startup. At 21 I graduated from the #2 finance school in the country... with a degree in Information Systems. At 22 I started work as a Business Technology consultant with Deloitte. And six months ago, I decided to dive in neck deep and become a full stack web developer.
